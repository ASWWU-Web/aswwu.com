Here at the Tread Shed we tune and repair student bikes and boards for free. The student only pays for parts, if needed.
We can fix flats and replace tubes, tune brakes, adjust derailleurs, replace parts, and more. We also clean longboard bearings. During the winter we sharpen and wax skis and snowboards.
