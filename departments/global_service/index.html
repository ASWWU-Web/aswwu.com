ASWWU Global Service

The Global Service Department is just like any other ASWWU department - we are here to improve the quality of life of WWU students. We aim to do so by giving students a chance to help make the world a better place. Our projects unite students, local residents and businesses, and the extended Adventist church in a spirit of greater community with a people and a need elsewhere in the world.
