One of These

Last year's One of These project raised $38,648.31 of its $35,000 goal. The funds are being used to open and operate Bangka Island Adventist School, a National Plus school to be started on Bangka Island, in the Bangka-Belitung province of Indonesia.


To Build A Home

To Build a Home raised over $100,000 to build an orphanage in Uganda. This year, three WWU students are currently serving as missionaries at the orphanage.

Mission Mozambique

Mission Mozambique provided $86,000 for new and sustainable water sources in Mozambique, Africa.
