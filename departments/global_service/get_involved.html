Get Involved

Here are a few ways you can get involved:

For Online Donations

  1. Go to wallawalla.edu.
  2. Click the red “Donate" button at the top right.
  3. Choose method for giving.
  4. Click “Donate Now” under “Missions and Ministries” and beside the heart icon.
  5. Fill out your information and select the box on the "ASWWU GLOBAL Missions Fund“ Line
  6. Click continue, and confirm your donation.

Send a Check

Write a check to the order of Walla Walla University.
In the memo line, write “ASWWU Global Service."
  Mail all checks to:
  WWU Office of Advancement: ASWWU Global Service
  204 South College Avenue
  College Place, WA 99324
