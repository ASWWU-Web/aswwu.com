Get Involved

Here are a few ways you can get involved:

For Online Donations

We have to options for online giving. The first and recommended method is through the official website of Walla Walla University, which offers a secure and fee-free way to donate. To donate through this site, follow the instructions outlined below.
  1. Go to wallawalla.edu
  2. Click on the icon that says, “Give a Gift”
  3. Click on the blue box that says, “Give Now”
  4. Click on the bubble that says, “Other”
  5. In the dropdown menu that appears, select “ASWWU Global Service”
  6. Fill out the rest of the form according to your preferences.

Our second option for online giving is through crowdrise.com, a site that offers a simple and secure way to donate. However, the site does take up to 8.9 % of the donation as a fee. To donate through this site, click the link below.
https://www.crowdrise.com/ASWWUServiceOutreach/fundraiser/wallawallauniversity

Send a Check

Write a check to the order of Walla Walla University.
In the memo line, write “ASWWU Global Service."
